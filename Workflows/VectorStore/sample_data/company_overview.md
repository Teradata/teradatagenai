# TechVision Solutions - Company Overview

## Mission Statement
Empowering businesses through innovative technology solutions that drive digital transformation and operational excellence.

## Company History
Founded in 2018, TechVision Solutions has grown from a startup to a leading provider of enterprise software solutions. Our journey:

- **2018**: Company founded with 5 employees
- **2019**: Launched CloudSync Pro, first major product
- **2020**: Expanded to European market, reached 50 employees
- **2021**: Introduced DataGuard Analytics, secured Series B funding
- **2022**: Opened Asia Pacific office, 200+ employees
- **2023**: SmartFlow Automation launch, 500+ enterprise customers
- **2024**: AI integration across all products, 1000+ customers
